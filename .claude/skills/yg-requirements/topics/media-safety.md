# Topic — Media safety (8.3.6, 8.3.7)

Source: <https://yandex.ru/dev/games/doc/ru/requirements/8/3/6>

## Rules
- **8.3.6** Safe, ethical, suitable for all ages, compliant with service rules.
- **8.3.7** No other prohibited materials.

Yandex reserves the right to restrict display even when no specific rule is violated.
