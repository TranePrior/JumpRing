# Topic — Screenshots & video materials (5.1.1.x)

Source: <https://yandex.ru/dev/games/doc/ru/requirements/5/1/1/2>

## Rules
- **5.1.1.1** No gameplay from other games in promo. No reused promo across different games.
- **5.1.1.2** Screenshots show real gameplay covering **≥70%** of the image.
- **5.1.1.3** Video materials show real gameplay covering **≥70%** of duration.

## Cannot check from code
These are draft-form assets. Mark ❓ Unverifiable and list manual checks:

1. Open each uploaded screenshot — gameplay area is ≥70%. Decorative borders / logos must not eat that share.
2. Trailer footage is from this game, not stock or another title.
3. No screenshot reused from another developer game.
4. No mock-up screens (e.g. concept art) — only real running gameplay.

## Related
- **5.6** Icon/cover cannot be a screenshot.
- **5.9** Black background only if it is part of the actual game.
